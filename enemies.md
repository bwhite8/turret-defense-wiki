## Enemy Scaling

All enemy stats increase with wave number:

### HP Scaling
- **Waves 1-50:** +18% per wave (Wave 50 = ~10x base HP)
- **Waves 51+:** Additional +2.4% exponential per wave

### Speed Scaling
- **Waves 1-75:** +4% per wave
- **Waves 76+:** Additional +1.2% exponential per wave
- **Cap:** Speed multiplier caps at 4.51x to prevent impossible speeds

### Shield Scaling
Shields scale at the same rate as HP.

### Reward Scaling
- **Waves 1-49:** +2% per wave
- **Waves 50+:** +8% per wave (accelerated to offset HP scaling)

## Enemy Count Per Wave

- **Base:** 25 + (wave x 8) enemies
- **Cap:** Maximum 100 spawned, 120 total with death spawns
- **Early waves (1-5):** 50% reduction
- **Recovery waves (after boss):** 40% reduction

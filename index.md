## Game Overview

**Objective:** Defend your reactor core from waves of enemy drones. Each enemy that reaches the core costs you lives. Lose all lives and it's game over!

**Core Gameplay Loop:**
1. **Build turrets** to defend paths to your core
2. **Survive waves** of increasingly difficult enemies
3. **Earn credits** from kills and wave completion bonuses
4. **Upgrade turrets** to increase damage, fire rate, and range
5. **Prestige** at wave 50+ to earn permanent bonuses
6. **Progress** through research, achievements, and permanent upgrades

**Starting Resources:**
- **1,500 credits** (increases with prestige)
- **20 lives** (increases with prestige and upgrades)

## What's New in Your Run?

Each game session, you can:
- Build from 10 different turret types
- Face 19 enemy types (unlocking as you progress through waves)
- Use consumable powerups from the Armory
- Earn credits through kills, wave bonuses, and interest
- Progress through increasingly difficult waves

Between games, you:
- Earn prestige points based on your score and waves reached
- Unlock permanent upgrades that boost future runs
- Research new turrets and abilities in the Research Lab
- Collect achievements for additional prestige points

## Quick Tips for New Players

1. **Start with Pulse Cannons** - They're cheap and reliable
2. **Don't skip upgrades** - Level 4-6 turrets have major power spikes
3. **Let interest build** - 5% interest on your credits each wave (capped)
4. **Block paths wisely** - Force enemies to walk through your turrets
5. **Prestige at wave 50+** - Permanent bonuses are worth resetting
